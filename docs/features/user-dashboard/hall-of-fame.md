# Hall of Fame

The Hall of Fame is a public gallery showcasing players who have completed challenges and earned their stickers. It's a social proof feature that celebrates achievements and motivates other players.

## What It Shows

Each completed challenge displays:
- The player's name
- The challenge they completed
- The sticker they earned
- When they completed it

The gallery is organized by challenge, so visitors can see who has completed each one.

## How Players Get Listed

Players appear in the Hall of Fame automatically when they complete a challenge (all puzzles solved). A sticker record is created and their name is added to the gallery.
