# Home / Landing Page

The home page is the public-facing landing page that introduces visitors to the Chess Puzzle Challenge platform and encourages them to browse challenges.

## Sections

### Hero

The main banner with:
- A headline about solving puzzles and earning medals.
- A call-to-action button to browse challenges.
- A "How it works" link.
- Social proof showing player count.

### Featured Challenges

A preview of available challenges with:
- Challenge name and difficulty level.
- Puzzle count and medal badge.
- Pricing (USD and MYR).
- Link to view details.

### How It Works

A step-by-step explanation of the process:
1. Choose a challenge.
2. Solve chess puzzles.
3. Earn your physical medal.
